Final Fantasy V
Final Fantasy V is the story of Bartz and his adventure to protect the crystals from an entity known as ExDeath, who plans to escape his prison and utilise the power of The Void.

Characters

Bartz
- An adventurer who becomes entagled in the fate of the world once he meets Lenna.


Lenna
- The daughter of the King Tycoon, who is missing. Lenna searches for him and meets up with Bartz.


Galuf
- An old man with amnesia, he only knows he has to get to a place called the Wind Shrine.


Faris
- A pirate captain who meets the party when they try sneak aboard the pirate ship.


Krile
- Galuf's granddaughter.
