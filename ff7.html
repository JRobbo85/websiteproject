Final Fantasy VII
Final Fantasy VII tells the tale of Cloud Strife, a mercenary who joins up with a group of eco-terrorists called Avalanche who want to stop a mega corporation called Shinra Electric Power Company from draining the planets life blood.

Cloud, Avalanche and others set off on a journey that will lead them to Sephiroth, an ex SOLDIER from Shinra who went missing 5 years ago under mysterious circumstances, and will eventually decide the fate of Gaia.

Characters

Cloud
- A former Soldier 1st Class turned mercenary.


Aerith
- The last remaining Ancient, the original stewards of the planet.


Barret
- Cloud's childhood friend, bar owner and member of Avalanche.


Red XIII
- A non human test subject found in the Shinra building.


Cait Sith
- A robot cat seated upon a giant stuffed Mog.


Cid Highwind
- A Shinra pilot who dreams of being the first man into space.


Vincent
- A former Turk (Shinra Special Ops) with a traumatic past, now infused with the power of Chaos.


Yuffie
- A ninja from the far away land of Wutai, who has a compulsion to steal all the Materia she can.
