Final Fantasy IX
The story of Final Fantasy IX follows a thief named Zidane Tribal, a member of a thief troupe who is tasked to kidnap the princess of Alexandria, Garnet Til Alexandros XVII.

He teams up with her and a team of allies to stop Queen Brahne and her ally Kuja from waging war with an army of black mages.

Characters

Zidane
- A thief and member of the Tantalus Theater Troupe.


Vivi
- A young black mage.


Garnet
- The Princess of Alexandria.


Steiner
- The leader of the Knights of Pluto and a noble knight of Alexandria.


Freya
- A Burmecian Dragon Knight.


Quina
- An apprentice gourmand who travels the world with the party looking for new foods.


Amarant
- A bandit who joins Zidane to learn where his power comes from.


Eiko
- A young summoner, maybe the last of her tribe.
