Final Fantasy VI
Final Fantasy VI is the story of Terra, The Gestahlian Empire and a resistance group known as The Returners.

The Returners are struggling against the magitech might of the Gestahlian Empire and look to gain the power of magic to aid them in their fight.

But the key to both sides seems to be a woman named Terra, who has a connection to a mysterious group of beings called Espers.

Characters

Terra
- A girl trained to be a weapon by the Gestahlian Empire.


Locke
- A treasure hunter and rebel sympathizer.


Edgar
- The King of Figaro who claims allegiance to the empire but supports the Returners on the side.


Sabin
- Edgars brother who's live revolves around honing his martial prowess.


Cyan
- A knight of the Kingdom of Doma.


Gau
- A feral child who lives on the wilds of the Veldt.


Celes
- A general of the Gestahlian Empire.


Setzer
- An airship pilot and gambler.


Shadow
- A mysterious mercenary who will work for anyone, for the right price.


Relm
- A painter from the town of Thamasa


Strago
- Relm's grandfather and one of the few remaining Magi in the world.


Mog
- A talking Moogle from the Narshe Mines.


Umaro
- A Yeti who only answers to Mog.


Gogo
- A master of the art of mimicry.
